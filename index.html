Unhurried Run Club

Welcome to the unhurried run club, a casual run club based in NYC. The club meets once a month on its second Saturday.

Next event: January 11, 2025 ☀️

Location

For this inaugural run, we will be meeting at Bowling Green Park at 10am next to the fountain. We will run along the hudson river park and end at pier 57 for coffee and snacks. The route is approximately 3.25 miles.
