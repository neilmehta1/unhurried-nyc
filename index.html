Unhurried Run Club

Welcome to the unhurried run club, a casual run club based in NYC. The club meets once a month on its second Saturday.

Next event: March 8, 2025 ⛅️

Location

For our February run, we will be meeting at Bowling Green Park at 11am next to the fountain†. We will run along the hudson river park and end at the chelsea market for coffee and snacks. The route is approximately 3.5 miles long.

Things to know

  • Please aim to arrive at 11am. The run will start shortly thereafter. I will be waiting by the meeting point at 10:55. You can text me your ETA in the morning so we don't leave without you.
  • The pace will be a slow jog (unhurried!).
  • Stick around after the run to celebrate with coffee/bagels/pastries.
  • Bring your friends!
  • The runs are all-weather‡ so please dress appropriately.
  • Run with at least one other person. Ensure no one is left behind.
  • Respect the road, other pedestrians, and traffic. Help the runners behind you avoid obstacles.
  • Please stay home if you're sick or injured.
